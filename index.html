Overview

Our Mission is to help make a change in this world through a fun way. For every 5 minutes spent playing any game on our website 10 cents will be donated to a charity (it adds up!) or for every survey question answered 5 cents will be donated to a charity. So go have fun! Play a game! Also if you would like to donate a game please contact us! The more games the better! Make sure to share the site with your friends so that we can help make a change in a fun way!
